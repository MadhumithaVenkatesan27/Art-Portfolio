About Me

Meet Madhumitha - The Artist Behind Pretty Stories

Hello! I'm Madhumitha Venkatesan, the heart behind Pretty Stories. I'm an artist, journaler, and storyteller who finds peace in patterns and joy in creativity. Through mandala art, painting, journaling, and DIY crafts, I express the little things in life that bring comfort, calm, and meaning.

What began as a personal journey of mindfulness and self-expression has now blossomed into a space where I share my creations, thoughts, and inspirations with the world. I believe even the simplest art can hold deep meaning. Whether it's a simple doodle or a detailed mandala, every piece I create is made with intention and heart.

Pretty Stories is my cozy little online studio, a collection of everything I love. Here, you'll find:

  • Mandala Art that reflects balance, peace, and creativity
  • Painting that brings emotions and stories to life through colors and brushstrokes
  • Journaling Ideas to help you reflect, heal, and grow
  • Crafts & DIY Projects that inspire playfulness and beauty
  • Stories behind the art because every creation has a soul

When I’m not creating, I love sipping coffee, walking with my dog, or getting inspired by nature and music. I’m endlessly fascinated by how colors, lines, and words can tell stories and I’m here to share those with you.

Thank you for being here. Whether you're an artist, a journal lover, or just someone looking for a little inspiration, I hope you find a piece of peace here. Let's keep creating pretty stories together!

With love,
Madhumitha
